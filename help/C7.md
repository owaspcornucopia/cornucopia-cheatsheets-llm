# C7 — Inadequate Audit Trail

## Threat

Mwengu's actions cannot be investigated because there is no adequate, accurately time-stamped record of security events, or there is no full audit trail, or these can be altered or deleted by Mwengu, or there is no centralised logging service.

## How This Applies

The C7 card describes actions that cannot be investigated because there is no adequate audit trail, no full record of security events, or because logs can be altered or deleted.

As covered by the Ace cards, the logging concern is valid and is already comprehensively covered by the Ace (A) cards in each category:

- [VEA](/help/VEA.md) — cannot detect attacks against input validation
- [ATA](/help/ATA.md) — cannot detect authentication attacks
- [SMA](/help/SMA.md) — cannot detect session management attacks
- [AZA](/help/AZA.md) — cannot detect authorisation bypass

Adding C7 as a separate applicable threat may seem redundant, but it's still applicable. The absence of logging and auditability is the same underlying issue described from four different angles by those cards.
